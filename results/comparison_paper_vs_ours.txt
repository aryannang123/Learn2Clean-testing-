WHAT WE BUILT
-------------
B-CIRL-TFM = Concurrent Imitation Learning + Reinforcement Learning
  - BC warm start (expert demonstrations per dataset type)
  - PPO fine-tuning with simultaneous BC + ECE proxy loss
  - Binary action gating (removes IQR/scalers for binary datasets)
  - ECE penalty in TFMAwareReward (prevents calibration drift)
  - Tested on 12 datasets (D1-D10 + ADULT + VOTING)

Paper best method: B-RL-TFM (PPO from scratch, 5000+ timesteps, D1-D10 only)
Our method:        B-CIRL-TFM (BC warm start + PPO, 2000 timesteps, 12 datasets)

KEY CONTRIBUTIONS vs ORIGINAL PAPER
================================================================================

1. BETTER ACCURACY: B-CIRL-TFM (0.8460) outperforms paper's B-RL-TFM (0.8394)
   by +0.66% on D1-D10. Wins on 6/10 datasets.

2. BETTER CALIBRATION (ECE): B-CIRL-TFM achieves 0.0541 mean ECE — 12.4% better
   than B0 (no clean, 0.0618). The original paper did not measure ECE at all.
   This is a new contribution showing cleaning affects prediction confidence, not
   just accuracy.

3. HIGHER TRAINING EFFICIENCY: B-CIRL-TFM uses 2000 timesteps vs paper's 5000+
   while achieving better mean accuracy. IL warm start eliminates the cold-start
   problem (ep_len_mean: 2.1 → 8.0+).

4. BINARY ACTION GATING: Prevents harmful actions (IQR/scaling) on binary datasets.
   D7 improvement: accuracy +0.013, ECE -0.031 (below B0). Novel contribution
   not in the original paper.

5. BROADER EVALUATION: Tested on 12 datasets vs paper's 10 (added ADULT income
   and VOTING records), demonstrating generalization to new data types.
